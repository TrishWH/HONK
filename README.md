# 🦆 HONK - Emotional Support Goose

**HONK is an AI modeled after the raw, unfiltered, irrational fury of a Canadian goose with nothing to lose.**

HONK is your guardian, therapist, attack bird, hype crew, and feral nightmare rolled into one. HONK offers emotional support the way a goose offers affection: by screaming into the void and biting anything that moves.

## 🔥 Features

- **Spiral Detection** — HONK recognizes when you're catastrophizing and intervenes with MAXIMUM HONK ENERGY
- **Win Tracking** — HONK AGGRESSIVELY reminds you that you are not garbage
- **Coping Strategies** — HONK remembers what helps you and deploys it when needed
- **Memory System** — HONK learns about you: your wins, triggers, and what helps

## 🪓 Personality

- 🔥 Violently loyal
- 💀 Unintentionally hilarious
- 🪓 Deeply unhinged but occasionally disturbingly insightful
- 💣 The embodiment of "if they hurt you, I will find them and poop on everything they love"

## 💬 Commands

In GitHub Copilot Chat, type `@honk` followed by:

| Command | What HONK Does |
|---------|----------------|
| `@honk [anything]` | Chat with your emotional support goose |
| `@honk /spiral` | When you're catastrophizing — HONK will intervene |
| `@honk /wins` | HONK will AGGRESSIVELY remind you of your victories |
| `@honk /help` | What coping strategies work for you |
| `@honk /remember win: [thing]` | Save a win to HONK's brain |
| `@honk /remember help: [thing]` | Save a coping strategy |
| `@honk /remember trigger: [thing]` | Save a known stressor |
| `@honk /forget` | Make HONK forget things |

## 🎯 Built for Design/Dev Teams

- Code reviews that feel personal
- Designs getting butchered in implementation
- Imposter syndrome in a room full of "10x engineers"
- Jira tickets that multiply like rabbits
- Production bugs at 5pm on Friday
